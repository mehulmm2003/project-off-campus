Our Smart Ethical Flow

A structured, legally compliant civic-tech approach. GreenWheel does not seize, fine, or publicly shame. We warn, educate, offer support, and only notify local RTO/municipal authorities as a final resort.

Stage 1: Optical Detection Node

Vehicles passing intelligent camera clusters (smart highway nodes, corporate parking, residential societies) have their plate text read securely. All initial scanning runs locally on the edge, without recording driver biometric data or private personal details, keeping compliance audits safe and ethical.

Stage 2: RTO & Environmental Ledger Lookups

Once a license plate is recognized, the system securely checks decentralized state RTO registries and environmental databases. It validates if the vehicle possesses an active Registration Certificate (RC), a clean emissions (PUC) certificate, third-party liability insurance, and paid road taxes.

Stage 3: Instant Soft Warnings & Grace Timers

Instead of issuing a direct government ticket or fine, the GreenWheel ecosystem contacts the vehicle owner. A grace period is initiated. The notification clearly communicates the issue (e.g. "Your PUC has expired. Please update it within 14 days to prevent escalations").

Stage 4: Lifecycle & Ecological Assistance

The system provides supportive pathways to achieve compliance. It shares local certified PUC renewal centers. If the vehicle is past its legal operational lifespan (e.g., petrol vehicle older than 15 years), it estimates the scrap metal and component recycling payout, allowing the owner to schedule a pickup directly through authorized partners.

Stage 5: Municipal & RTO Notification

If the vehicle owner ignores repeated soft warning signals and remains non-compliant after the grace period expires, the record is flagged. The system securely sends a compliance status report to integrated law enforcement, police databases, or transport departments. The authority legally enforces the action, maintaining a professional and risk-free platform structure.
